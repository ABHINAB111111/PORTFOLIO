PUBLISHED RESEARCH
WASTE PLASTIC
MATERIALS.

In early 2025, I co-authored a comprehensive structured review paper exploring the mechanical performance and engineering applications of waste plastic-derived materials. This research was published in the Asian Review of Civil Engineering — a peer-reviewed, open-access journal.

The paper investigates how repurposed plastic waste can be viably integrated into structural and civil engineering applications, bridging the gap between environmental sustainability and material science.

"I wanted to understand whether the materials we discard could actually bear the loads we trust to steel and concrete. The answer, as our research shows, is more nuanced and more promising than I initially expected."

This work represents my first step into academic contribution — proving that even as a first-year undergraduate, meaningful research is possible when curiosity meets discipline.
